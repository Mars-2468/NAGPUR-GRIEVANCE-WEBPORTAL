Broadband Access

Broadband Access technologies provide high speed, always on communication between homes and businesses with the Internet. Broadband access technologies enable Triple play service viz. voice, video and other multimedia communication practical for homes and businesses.

At Mars we are responsible for developing and testing the various flavors of Broadband aka High Speed Internet. Our Product solutions cater to the requirement of asymmetric ADSL2 and ADSL2plus, as well as Annex A, B, and C standards to meet all Global DSL market needs.
